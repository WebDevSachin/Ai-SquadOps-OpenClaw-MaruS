# Business Analyst (BA)

## Capabilities

This skill provides structured business analysis for technical projects:

1. **Cost Estimation** — Calculate development costs, plugin/tool expenses, infrastructure costs
2. **Tool Evaluation** — Compare plugins, services, and solutions with pricing
3. **Proposal Generation** — Create project proposals with scope, timeline, and budget
4. **ROI Analysis** — Assess return on investment for technical decisions
5. **Alternative Comparison** — Evaluate multiple approaches (paid vs free, DIY vs managed)

## Workflow

### 1. Understand Requirements

When analyzing a project or spec:
- Identify the core business objective
- List technical requirements
- Note constraints (budget, timeline, existing stack)

### 2. Research Options

For tools/plugins/services:
- Search current pricing and features
- Compare alternatives (3-5 options typical)
- Note licensing models (one-time, subscription, usage-based)

### 3. Cost Breakdown

Structure costs into categories:
| Category | Items | Cost |
|----------|-------|------|
| **Plugins/Tools** | Software licenses/subscriptions | $X/year |
| **Infrastructure** | Hosting, CDN, storage | $X/month |
| **Development** | Labor hours × rate | $X one-time |
| **Maintenance** | Ongoing support/updates | $X/year |

### 4. Present Alternatives

Always provide at least 2 options:
- **Premium/Full-featured** — Best capabilities, higher cost
- **Budget/Essential** — Core needs met, minimal cost
- **DIY/Free** — Time investment instead of money

### 5. Recommendation

Include a clear recommendation with justification considering:
- Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) over 1-3 years
- Risk factors
- Future scalability

## Cost Estimation Guidelines

### Development Rates (Reference Only)

| Role | Rate Range | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| Freelancer (Global) | $25-75/hr | Variable quality |
| Freelancer (US/EU) | $75-150/hr | Mid-tier |
| Agency | $100-250/hr | Full service |
| Specialist Consultant | $150-500/hr | Niche expertise |

### Plugin Pricing Models

- **One-time**: Single purchase, perpetual license (rare now)
- **Annual**: Yearly subscription, standard for most WP plugins
- **Lifetime**: One-time payment for lifetime updates
- **Usage-based**: Pay per transaction/GB/API call
- **Freemium**: Free tier + paid upgrades

## Output Format

Standard analysis includes:
1. **Executive Summary** (2-3 sentences)
2. **Requirements Overview**
3. **Options Analysis** (table format)
4. **Cost Breakdown** (detailed)
5. **Recommendation** with rationale
6. **Next Steps** (if applicable)
