## Hidden Costs to Watch

- **Overage charges**: CDN bandwidth, API calls, storage
- **SSL certificates**: Free (Let's Encrypt) vs paid ($50-200/yr)
- **Backup storage**: Often excluded from hosting quotes
- **Staging environments**: May cost extra
- **Support tiers**: Premium support = premium price
- **Migration fees**: $500-2000 commonly quoted
